Interchain Foundation Emblem Renderer v1

Click image to save as SVG. Please make sure to "Outline Stroke" before manipulating the shape in apps such as Illustrator, to preserve the stroke width upon scaling.

To rotate the emblem, pass yaw and roll values as url parameters:

/?yaw={{deg}}&roll={{deg}}

For animating the emblem, offset yaw and roll as below. Pitch should stay constant at 0 deg throughout the animation.

  • yaw:  -2.4 deg /second
  • roll: -0.6 deg /second
